What is CODEFLARE?

  1. CODEFLARE 2025 – The Ultimate AI Buildathon (Code from Home) is an AI innovation challenge organized by Code Club, BVEC for college students to build real-world projects or prototypes. The event encourages participants to think creatively and apply AI tools or features in their solutions, which will be considered a bonus point during evaluation.

  2. Participants can choose any one of the four problem statements and develop their solution using any preferred technology or framework. The event is open to individuals as well as teams of up to 2 members. The primary goal is to foster creativity, innovation, and hands-on problem-solving, giving students practical experience in developing impactful projects.

  3. For the final submission, participants must provide:
    1. A public GitHub repository containing the complete project.
    2. A PPT (8–10 slides) explaining the idea, workflow, AI integration, and challenges.
    3. A 3–4 minute video presentation describing the project, AI tools used, and development process.

    The submission deadline is 16th October 2025, 11:59 PM. Participants are encouraged to include a simple user interface (UI) or basic prototype demo for better evaluation by the judges.
